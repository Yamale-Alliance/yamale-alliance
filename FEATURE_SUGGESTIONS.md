# Feature Suggestions for Yamalé Legal Platform

## 📚 Library & Content Features

### Quick Wins
- [ ] **Law Comparison Tool** - Side-by-side comparison of laws across different African countries
- [ ] **Law Annotations** - Allow users to add personal notes/highlights to specific sections of laws
- [ ] **Bookmark/Favorites** - Save frequently accessed laws for quick retrieval
- [ ] **Law History** - Track recently viewed laws with quick access
- [ ] **Export Law Sections** - Download specific sections as PDF/DOCX with citations
- [ ] **Law Version History** - Show when laws were updated and view previous versions
- [ ] **Related Laws** - Suggest related laws based on content similarity or topic
- [ ] **Law Summaries** - AI-generated summaries of long legal documents
- [ ] **Citation Generator** - Generate proper legal citations in various formats (Bluebook, OSCOLA, etc.)
- [ ] **Print-Friendly View** - Optimized print layout for laws

### Advanced
- [ ] **Law Change Tracking** - Get notified when laws you follow are amended
- [ ] **Multi-Language Support** - Translate laws between English, French, Arabic, Portuguese
- [ ] **Law Commentary** - Expert-written commentary on important laws
- [ ] **Case Law Integration** - Link relevant court cases to specific law sections
- [ ] **Regulatory Updates Feed** - RSS/email feed of new laws and amendments by country
